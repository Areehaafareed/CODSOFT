Deepika Padukone

She is an Indian actress who works predominantly in Hindi films. One of the highest-paid actresses in India, her accolades include three FilmFare Awards.

Early Life

Padukone was born on 5 January 1986 in Copenhagen, Denmark, to Konkani-speaking parents.She was educated at Bangalore's Sophia High School and completed her pre-university education at Mount Carmel College.

Career

Early in her career, Padukone gained recognition with a television commercial for the soap Liril and modelled for various other brands and products.[14][15] In 2005, she made her runway debut at the Lakme Fashion Week for designer Suneet Varma and won Model of the Year at the Kingfisher Fashion Awards.[16][17] Padukone's fame increased when she appeared in a popular print campaign for the 2006 Kingfisher Calendar
